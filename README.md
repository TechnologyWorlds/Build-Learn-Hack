# Technology World 🚀

Welcome to Technology World! This is a hub for makers, engineers, and curious minds. Here, we explore the exciting world of electronics, coding, and hardware hacking.

## What you will learn here:
* **Arduino:** From basic blinking LEDs to complex sensor-based automation systems.
* **Raspberry Pi:** Discover how to turn this credit-card-sized computer into a media server, retro gaming console, or a home automation brain.
* **Hardware Hacking:** Detailed guides and "under the hood" explanations of how everyday gadgets work.
* **Prototyping:** Step-by-step tutorials to take your idea from a rough sketch to a functional prototype.

## Our Mission
Just like a good engineering project, we believe that understanding *how* things work is the most important part. We don't just give you code; we explain the logic, the physics, and the design choices behind every project.

## Get Started
Check out the folders in this repository to find projects, code samples, and circuit diagrams. Feel free to explore, learn, and build along with us!
